Current Project

Recently, two friends and I began developing a mobile app. We are creating a period tracker so women can track data relevant to their periods and get predictions on when their next period will occur. We identified a market for this app because most of the period tracker apps have limited functionality and a poor user experience. I am currently teaching myself Swift as well as mobile development techniques and best practices to complete the iOS version of our app.

Dr. Coffee

As part of Yelp's internal hackathon, another intern and I augmented a Mr. Coffee to be controlled over wifi. We attached a photon to the coffee maker so we could turn the coffee maker on and off over the cloud. We also added sensors to track the temperature of the coffee and the amount remaining in the pot. A web app allowed users to view this data, start and stop the brew process, and see the brew time remaining from anywhere in the office. Checkout Dr. Coffee's Github.
